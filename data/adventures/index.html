About Adventurers Club

Welcome to the SDA Adventurers Club, a ministry designed for children ages 6-9 that encourages kids to develop a Christ-like character while having fun through outdoor activities, crafts, and community service.

Our program helps children build relationships with Jesus, their families, and their peers through a variety of engaging activities that promote physical, mental, and spiritual growth.

Each meeting is filled with exciting adventures, meaningful lessons, and opportunities to earn badges as they progress through the program. Our trained leaders provide a safe and nurturing environment where children can explore, learn, and grow.

Our Values

Our Core Values

We build character through these foundational principles that guide all our activities and interactions.

Service

We teach children the joy of serving others through community projects and helping those in need.

Nature Appreciation

Exploring God's creation helps children develop respect for the environment and all living things.

Spiritual Growth

Through Bible stories and devotionals, we nurture each child's relationship with Jesus.

Friendship

We create opportunities for children to build meaningful friendships in a positive environment.

Personal Achievement

Our badge program encourages children to set and accomplish goals at their own pace.

Joyful Learning

We believe learning should be fun! Our activities engage children through play and discovery.
