Who We Are

We are a community of devoted women within the church, united by purpose, faith, and the calling to serve. Our department nurtures spiritual growth, fellowship, and active outreach through service and empowerment.

Our Mission

  • To inspire and support women to grow in Christ and reflect His love in every aspect of life.
  • To build a strong network of spiritual, emotional, and practical support for women of all ages.
  • To serve the church and community with compassion, courage, and grace.
