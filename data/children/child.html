Your Child's Safety Matters

We create fun environments with serious safeguards

Secure Check-In/Out

  • Digital check-in with printed name tags & parent receipts
  • Photo ID required for pickup (no exceptions)
  • Two-factor notification system for emergencies

Team Vetting

  • All volunteers background-checked annually
  • Minimum 2 adults present in every room
  • Child protection training every 6 months

Health Standards

  • Professional cleaning between services
  • Wellness checks at check-in
  • First-aid certified staff in every wing

Safe Environments

  • Secure-access children's wing
  • Live camera monitoring (staff only)
  • Emergency evacuation drills quarterly

Have questions? Visit our Safety Desk or email safety@church.org
