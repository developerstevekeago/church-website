Youth aren't just the future - they're the NOW

Welcome to the Youth Ministry of Saika West SDA Church – a vibrant, dynamic space where young people are empowered to grow spiritually, mentally, and socially. Our goal is to create a Christ-centered environment that nurtures faith, encourages creativity, and builds lasting friendships. We believe the youth are not just the future of the church – they are the now, and this is their space to connect, learn, and lead.

🧭

Our Mission

Our mission is to guide young people into a deeper relationship with God by providing opportunities for worship, discipleship, service, and fellowship. We aim to help youth discover their purpose, develop their gifts, and become influential leaders both in the church and the world.

📆 Weekly & Monthly Activities

We host a range of weekly and monthly activities tailored to uplift and engage the youth. These include Sabbath afternoon discussions, Bible studies, praise and worship sessions, talent showcases, sports tournaments, outreach programs, and game nights. Each activity is designed to make faith practical and relevant to everyday life, while also giving youth a chance to express themselves and have fun.

🗣️ Youth Leadership & Mentorship

We believe in developing strong, spiritual leaders. Our youth department is run by a passionate team of young leaders and mentors who are dedicated to serving and guiding others. Through mentorship programs, peer-led initiatives, and training workshops, we equip youth to take ownership of their faith and responsibilities in the church and community.

👥

Mentorship Programs

  • One-on-one spiritual guidance
  • Intergenerational connections
  • Personal growth plans
🌟

Peer Leadership

  • Youth-led worship services
  • Ministry team positions
  • Outreach program leadership
📚

Training Workshops

  • Public speaking coaching
  • Bible study techniques
  • Ministry management
